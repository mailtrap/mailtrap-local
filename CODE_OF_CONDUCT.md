# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates — contributors,
maintainers, and users alike — regardless of background or identity.

## Our standard

We adopt the **[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1** as our code of conduct. The full text is published at:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

In short: be kind, assume good faith, give and accept constructive feedback
gracefully, and keep discussion focused on the work. Behavior that makes others
feel unwelcome or unsafe is not acceptable in any project space (issues, pull
requests, discussions, or any other channel).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to
